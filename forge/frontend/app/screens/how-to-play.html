❓ HOW TO PLAY
🎮 GAME MODES
🎯
SOLO Play any topic on your own. Chase your best score and earn coins & trophies based on how well you do — no one else needed.
🚀
CLASSIC Create a room or join one with a 4-character code. Everyone answers the same questions live — fastest correct answers score the most. Winner takes the coin pot.
⚔️
TEAM Lock the room, split into two teams, and each team picks a topic. The randomizer decides which topic gets played — the team with the higher combined score wins.
💰 THE ECONOMY
🪙
COINS Your spendable currency. Earn them by winning games. Spend them on Classic/Team room entry fees and on buying generation tickets in the Shop.
🏆
TROPHIES Your rank. Trophies are never spent — only won or lost based on performance. More trophies, higher rank on the leaderboard.
🎟️
GENERATION TICKETS Quick Pick topics (Movies, Science, Cricket, etc.) are always free — no ticket, no sign-in needed. Typing your own custom AI topic costs 1 ticket. You get 3 free every day.
🔥 DAILY REWARD & STREAKS
🎁
DAILY REWARD Sign in every day to claim a growing reward of coins and tickets. It runs on a 7-day cycle — Day 7 is the jackpot, then it starts over.
🔥
STREAKS Play at least one game a day to keep your streak going. Miss a day and it resets — turn on Streak Reminders in Settings so you never forget.

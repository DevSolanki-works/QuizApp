Forge Trivia Topic Guide

Forge works best when the host gives the AI a clear subject, audience, and scope. This guide collects practical topic ideas and prompt patterns that make AI-generated quiz rooms more fun, fair, and useful.

Try a Topic Read the Rules

How to Choose a Strong Topic

A good Forge topic is specific enough to produce accurate questions but broad enough to support ten rounds. Instead of asking for "movies", ask for a decade, franchise, country, award category, or theme. Instead of asking for "science", specify the school level, branch, or concept area.

Better Topics

  • Indian cricket World Cup moments from 1983 to 2023
  • Beginner Python programming concepts
  • Marvel Cinematic Universe phases 1 to 3
  • Class 10 biology: human body systems

Weak Topics

  • Sports
  • Computer
  • History
  • Random hard questions

Topic Ideas by Situation

Friends and Parties

Use pop culture, music, sports, or shared memories. Keep the scope familiar so everyone has a chance.

2000s Bollywood songs and movie trivia

Classroom Review

Choose a chapter or exam unit. Easy mode gives students enough time to think and discuss after each reveal.

Class 9 physics: motion and forces

Office Team Breaks

Pick neutral, inclusive themes that do not rely on inside jokes. Team Mode works well here.

General business trivia for beginners

Family Game Night

Mix nostalgia with easy facts. Use Medium for adults and Easy if younger players are joining.

Famous cartoons and family movies

Solo Practice

Use tighter topics when learning. Repeat the same area with different difficulty settings to train speed.

Machine learning basics: supervised learning

Final Showdown

For a close room, switch to Hard mode and pick a balanced general-knowledge theme.

World landmarks, inventions, and records

Prompt Patterns That Work

  • Audience + topic: "General knowledge for Indian college students".
  • Time period + subject: "Football World Cups from 1998 to 2022".
  • Level + syllabus: "Beginner JavaScript arrays and functions".
  • Franchise + range: "Harry Potter books 1 to 4".
  • Comparison theme: "Famous inventors and what they invented".

Topics to Avoid

Forge is built for entertainment and learning. Do not use topics that ask for private personal data, hateful content, explicit content, dangerous instructions, medical diagnosis, legal advice, or anything intended to mislead other players. The game includes input filtering, but the best experience starts with a fair host topic.
